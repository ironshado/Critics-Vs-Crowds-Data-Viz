# Critics-Vs-Crowds-Data-Viz

What does "best" actually mean when critical consensus and commercial performance tell completely different stories? This data viz maps 30 years of that tension — across film and gaming, by genre, by decade, by dollar. Built with vanilla JS, Canvas API, and Chart.js, designed in an obsidian and safety orange system.


A few stories this surfaces immediately:

The 2000s were when gaming's critic picks and crowd picks started dramatically diverging — the Call of Duty era vs. auteur games like BioShock and Journey
Oscar dramas have been consistently low-grossers since 2009 (Moonlight, CODA, Birdman) while action dominates box office
2003 and 2018 are the rare "consensus years" where critic and crowd agreed on both a movie and a game (Return of the King and Red Dead Redemption 2)
